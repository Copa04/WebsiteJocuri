Cele mai populare genuri de jocuri video pe PC sunt:

  1. FPS (First-Person Shooter) - ex: Doom, Counter-Strike, Half-Life
  2. RPG (Role-Playing Game) - ex: Diablo, The Witcher, Baldur's Gate
  3. RTS (Real-Time Strategy) - ex: StarCraft, Warcraft, Age of Empires
  4. MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) - ex: Dota 2, League of Legends
  5. Sandbox - ex: Minecraft, Terraria
  6. Puzzle - ex: Tetris, Portal
